How to make a bic mac

(note that you shoud only do this in a McDonald's)

Just order one, duh!

But this is how they make it

Here what it should look like

Required ingredients

  • 1 Big Mac bun
  • 15ml of Big Mac sauce
  • 2 picles
  • 10g of onion
  • 2 45g beef patties
  • a slice of cheddar cheese
  • 28g of shredded lettuce

Stages of cooking one

  1. You should prepare all the ingredients and cook the patties for 40 seconds in a standard issue grill
  2. First, toast the insides of the buns that will be used
  3. Stack the middle and top pieces on eachother and have the bottom piece next to this tower
  4. For both sides, add sauce, on top of that onion and on top of that, lettuce
  5. For the bottom, add cheese, then a patty, For the top, add pickles and then the patty
  6. Move the middle piece with its ingredients on top of the bottom one and stack the top on that

    7. Voila you have completed your burger, now package it how you want
